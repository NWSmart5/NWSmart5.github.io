Safe & Responsible AI

Ethical, transparent, and secure AI adoption for manufacturing SMEs — aligned with Industry 5.0 human-centric principles.

Why Safe AI Matters

AI can transform predictive maintenance, quality control and automation — but must be implemented responsibly to avoid bias, data leaks and loss of human oversight.

Key Risks & Mitigation

  • Data leakage through AI tools
  • Bias in automated decisions
  • Lack of explainability
  • Over-reliance reducing human control

Best Practice Checklist

  • ✅ Start with small pilots
  • ✅ Perform AI ethics & impact assessments
  • ✅ Use explainable AI tools
  • ✅ Maintain human oversight
  • ✅ Train staff on responsible use

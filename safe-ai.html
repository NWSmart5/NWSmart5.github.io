Safe AI in Manufacturing

AI adoption in SMEs must be secure, transparent, and aligned with ethical and operational requirements.

Key Risks

  • Data leakage through AI tools
  • Bias in automated decisions
  • Lack of explainability

Best Practices

  • Use trusted AI platforms
  • Limit sensitive data input
  • Implement human oversight

Safe & Responsible AI

Ethical, transparent, and secure AI adoption aligned with Industry 5.0 human-centric principles.

Why Safe AI Matters

AI for predictive maintenance and automation must be implemented responsibly to avoid bias, data leaks, and loss of human oversight.

Best Practice Checklist

  • ✅ Start with small pilots
  • ✅ Conduct AI ethics assessments
  • ✅ Use explainable AI tools
  • ✅ Maintain human oversight
  • ✅ Train staff on responsible use

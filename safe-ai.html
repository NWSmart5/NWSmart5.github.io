Safe & Responsible AI

Ethical, transparent, and secure AI adoption aligned with Industry 5.0 human-centric principles.

Why Safe AI Matters for SMEs

AI enables predictive maintenance, quality control, and automation — but must be implemented responsibly to avoid bias, data leaks, and loss of human oversight.

Best Practice Checklist

  • ✅ Start with small pilot projects
  • ✅ Conduct AI ethics & impact assessments
  • ✅ Use explainable AI tools
  • ✅ Maintain meaningful human oversight
  • ✅ Train staff on responsible use

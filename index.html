Welcome to NWSmart5.0 Knowledge Hub

The UK manufacturing sector is transitioning to Industry 5.0 — a human-centric, sustainable and resilient future. However, many small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs) in the North West face significant barriers including technological complexity, limited accessible knowledge, and concerns around secure implementation.

This Knowledge Hub has been developed as part of the CyberFocus project to bridge these gaps. It provides free, practical resources on Cybersecurity, Safe & Responsible AI, and Digital Transformation — all tailored specifically for manufacturing SMEs.

Our Vision

To empower manufacturing SMEs in the North West to lead the next era of industrial transformation — one that is secure, smart, sustainable, and human-centric.

What We Offer

  • ✅ Free practical resources and toolkits
  • ✅ Interactive regional workshops on cybersecurity and safe AI
  • ✅ Bespoke digital transformation support for selected SMEs
  • ✅ Guidance aligned with Industry 5.0 principles

Upcoming Workshops

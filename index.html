Welcome to NWSmart5.0 Knowledge Hub

The UK manufacturing sector is entering Industry 5.0 — placing human-centricity, sustainability and resilience at the heart of technological progress. Many SMEs in the North West still face barriers such as technological complexity, skills gaps and uncertainty around secure implementation.

This Knowledge Hub is part of the CyberFocus project, funded to help manufacturing SMEs adopt digital technologies safely and responsibly.

Our Vision

To empower manufacturing SMEs in the North West to lead the next era of industrial transformation — one that is secure, smart, sustainable, and truly human-centric.

What We Offer

  • Free practical resources, toolkits and infographics
  • Interactive regional workshops on cybersecurity and safe AI
  • Bespoke digital transformation support for selected SMEs
  • Open-access Knowledge Hub for ongoing support

Our Partners

NWSmart5.0 is a collaborative project bringing together academia, industry and regional stakeholders to deliver real impact for manufacturing SMEs.

  • University of Salford – Academic leadership in AI, cybersecurity and digital transformation
  • Greater Manchester Chamber of Commerce (GMCC) – Industry engagement and SME outreach
  • Greater Manchester Institute of Technology (GMIoT) – Skills development and workforce support

This partnership ensures that all resources and activities are practical, relevant and aligned with the needs of Northwest manufacturers.

Upcoming Workshops

Workshop topics include:

  • Safe and responsible AI adoption

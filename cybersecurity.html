Cybersecurity for Manufacturing SMEs

Protect your factory floors, OT systems, supply chains and digital operations with practical, proven guidance from the NCSC and CyBOK.

Why Cybersecurity Matters for Manufacturing

Manufacturing is a high-value target for cybercriminals. Attacks on Operational Technology (OT) and Industrial Control Systems (ICS) can halt production, compromise safety, and cause significant financial loss.

According to the NCSC, manufacturing organisations continue to face elevated risks from ransomware, supply-chain attacks, and phishing. Strong cyber resilience is now essential for Industry 5.0 success.

Common Threats in Manufacturing

  • Ransomware – Can lock down production lines
  • Phishing & Social Engineering – Primary entry point for most attacks
  • Supply Chain Attacks – Targeting third-party suppliers
  • OT/IoT Vulnerabilities – Legacy equipment often unpatched
  • Insider Threats – Accidental or malicious

NCSC 10 Steps to Cyber Security

The National Cyber Security Centre’s 10 Steps to Cyber Security remains the gold standard for UK organisations. These are especially relevant for manufacturing SMEs.

  • ✅ Risk Management Regime
  • ✅ Secure Configuration
  • ✅ Network Security
  • ✅ Managing User Privileges
  • ✅ User Education and Awareness
  • ✅ Incident Management
  • ✅ Malware Prevention
  • ✅ Monitoring and Logging
  • ✅ Removable Media Controls
  • ✅ Home & Mobile Working

Recommendation: Start with Cyber Essentials certification — a government-backed scheme and a proven way to demonstrate good cyber hygiene to customers, insurers, and supply chain partners.

Securing Operational Technology (OT)

Manufacturing environments often use legacy systems that were never designed with internet connectivity in mind. Key actions include:

  • ✅ Network segmentation between IT and OT
  • ✅ Regular vulnerability assessments of ICS/SCADA systems
  • ✅ Patch management strategies for legacy equipment
  • ✅ Secure remote access (avoid default passwords)
  • ✅ Physical security of critical control systems

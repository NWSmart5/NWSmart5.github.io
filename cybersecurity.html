Cybersecurity for Manufacturing SMEs

Practical, NCSC-backed guidance to protect your OT systems, supply chains, and digital operations in the Industry 5.0 era.

Why Cybersecurity is Critical for Manufacturing

Manufacturing businesses are prime targets for cybercriminals. Attacks on Operational Technology (OT) and Industrial Control Systems can halt production, compromise safety, and cause major financial loss.

Strong cyber resilience is essential for secure Industry 5.0 transformation.

Common Threats in Manufacturing

  • Ransomware – Can lock down entire production lines
  • Phishing & Social Engineering – Most common entry point
  • Supply Chain Attacks
  • OT/IoT Vulnerabilities in legacy equipment
  • Denial of Service (DoS) attacks

Securing Operational Technology (OT)

  • Segment IT and OT networks
  • Secure remote access and remove default passwords
  • Regular patching and vulnerability assessments
  • Develop OT-specific incident response plans

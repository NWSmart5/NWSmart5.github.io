Cybersecurity for Manufacturing

Practical, CyBOK-aligned protection for factory floors, OT systems, and supply chains.

Key Threats in Manufacturing SMEs

Ransomware targeting production lines, phishing, supply-chain attacks, and insecure IoT/OT devices.

Recommended Actions

  • ✅ Implement NCSC 10 Steps to Cyber Security
  • ✅ Achieve Cyber Essentials certification
  • ✅ Secure remote access and OT networks
  • ✅ Regular backups and incident response planning
  • ✅ Staff awareness training (especially phishing)

Cybersecurity for Manufacturing

Practical protection for factory floors, OT systems, IoT and supply chains using NCSC tools and CyBOK.

Common Threats in Manufacturing

Ransomware, phishing, supply-chain attacks, and insecure connected devices are increasing risks to production.

Recommended Actions

  • ✅ Follow NCSC 10 Steps to Cyber Security
  • ✅ Work towards Cyber Essentials certification
  • ✅ Secure OT/IoT and remote access
  • ✅ Create backup and incident response plans
  • ✅ Run regular staff awareness training

Cybersecurity for Manufacturing

CyBOK-aligned practical guidance to protect your factory floor, OT systems, and supply chain.

Common Threats in Manufacturing SMEs

Ransomware, phishing, supply-chain attacks, and insecure IoT/OT devices are major risks to production continuity.

Recommended Actions

  • Follow NCSC 10 Steps to Cyber Security
  • Achieve Cyber Essentials certification
  • Secure OT/IoT and remote access
  • Regular backups and incident response planning
  • Staff awareness training
